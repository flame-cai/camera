XR · SLAM
H1 · XR local-floor viewer.y
WebXR local-floor reference space sets Y=0 at the detected floor. The viewer pose Y is then the direct camera height above floor. Uses ARCore/ARKit SLAM — continuous tracking, sub-centimetre accuracy.
height = viewerPose.transform.position.y
accuracy: ±2 cm · 60 fps · requires floor surface visible

Δh = 44330 × (1 − (P/P₀)^0.1903)

ML · All Models
H6 · ML depth — floor region average
All 4 ML models run in parallel. Each produces a depth map. Floor region (bottom 15% of frame) is averaged to give floor depth, converted to height. DepthPro and ZoeDepth give metric directly. DA2 models use a scale factor S calibrated from any metric anchor (XR or barometer).
h = S / floorAvgDisparity (DA2 models)
h = floorAvgDepth (DepthPro / ZoeDepth — metric direct)

H7 · Trigonometric h = D·sin(|dep|)
Cross-check method. Uses fused distance D and camera depression angle from gravity to compute height geometrically. Depression = asin(−gz / |g|) from gravity vector — no assumptions about phone orientation.
h = D_fused × |sin(asin(−gz/|g|))| · fusion weight: 1.5
