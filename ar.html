H7 · Trigonometric h = D · sin(|dep|)
Uses fused distance D and camera depression angle from gravity vector. Depression = asin(−gz / |g|) — purely geometric, no camera calibration. Acts as cross-check. Also includes visual horizon Sobel, accel integration, and DeviceOrientation fallback.
