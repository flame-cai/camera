Honesty note. H_real is exact only while the phone is upright (horizon on the centre line) with feet on flat ground in frame; the tilt badge guards that. Distance always needs a focal length — calibrate it. M1/M2/M3 share one geometry, so their agreement is a detection-quality check, not independent confirmation. The AR-depth method is independent of all that — it reads ARCore's metric depth at the centre reticle — but only runs on depth-capable devices. M4/M5/M6 and M8-phase-2 from the spec are omitted (browser inputs can't hit their claimed accuracy).
