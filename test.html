1Enter camera height above ground and subject inputs

⚠ Accuracy: Height ±3–6 cm, Weight ±5–12 kg, MUAC ±1–2 cm. Requires: plain background, known camera height, subject standing straight. Not for clinical diagnosis.

Most rear cameras: 65–75°. Wide-angle: 90–110°. Check your phone spec or leave default 69°.

CAPTURE RESOLUTION—
Height method: Full-body silhouette pixel height ÷ pixels-per-metre.
Scale (px/m): f_px = image_width / (2·tan(FOV/2)). Then at distance D: px/m = f_px / D.
Distance: From camera height H_c and ankle pixel position: D = H_c · f_px / (ankle_y − centre_y).
Weight: Lorentz formula (sex-adjusted) + shoulder/hip width deviations.
MUAC: Arm width in pixels × cm/px scale × π (diameter → circumference).
