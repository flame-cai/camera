Honesty note. H_real is a ratio of pixel offsets, so it is exact and metric with no focal length. Distance needs the camera's angular scale; with only H_cam that scale must come from EXIF (preferred) or an assumed FoV (then distance is scale-approx). M1=M4=ground geometry; M2 uses foot landmarks to avoid a +7% ankle bias; M3 uses the corrected pinhole form (the spec's was off by a double-angle); M5 parallax is gated and low-confidence because its baseline comes from drift-prone IMU integration. Voting reduces detection outliers, not shared geometric error — so confidence is built from detection quality, verticality, feet-margin and f-source, not from method agreement.
