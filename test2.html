Notes. H_real is a ratio of pixel offsets, so it is exact and metric without any focal length. Distance requires the camera's angular scale: with only the camera height it must come from EXIF (preferred) or, failing that, an assumed field of view (distance then marked scale-approx). M1 and M4 are the same ground-plane geometry; M2 uses foot landmarks to avoid a +7% ankle bias; M3 uses the corrected pinhole form; M5 (autofocus depth) is independent of focal length and of the upright assumption but depends on the camera exposing focus distance; voting reduces detection outliers, not shared geometric error, so confidence is derived from detection quality, verticality, feet-margin and focal-length source rather than from method agreement.
